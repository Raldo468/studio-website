How it works

Four steps from file to doorstep

01
Send your file

Email us your STL, OBJ, or STEP file — or just describe what you need. We'll handle the rest and confirm printability before anything starts.

02
Choose material

Pick from PLA+, PETG, Carbon Fiber, or TPU. Not sure? We'll recommend the right material for your use case — no upselling, just honest advice.

03
We print & inspect

Printed on the Creality K2 with CFS multi-color system. Every part is checked before it leaves our hands — we don't ship prints we wouldn't keep.

04
Ships to you

Packed and shipped within 48 hours. Local LA pickup also available. You'll get a photo before it ships — no surprises.
